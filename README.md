# Kuoloon Chong

**Product Designer / Design Engineer**

I'm a designer and engineer who builds AI agents. The problem I care about most is the balance between the deterministic harness and the non-deterministic model: how much to constrain it, what the agent remembers, when it acts, and how it shows its work. I think that balance is where good agents are won or lost.

## Selected work

**Akarii** · [www.akarii.ai](https://www.akarii.ai)
An AI-native team workspace. An AI teammate that observes team conversations, surfaces cited decisions, and executes work in the cloud. Built solo across product, design, and engineering.

**Omaru** · private alpha
An AI business partner inside a browser-based desktop OS that helps people turn their skills into a sellable service business. Co-founded; I lead onboarding, business setup, and the project lifecycle.

**Carbon** · [github.com/kyonyhk/carbon](https://github.com/kyonyhk/carbon)
A minimal agent harness built from scratch: a headless agent loop, four tools, and file-based sessions. The foundation I build other agents on.

**Graphite** · [github.com/kyonyhk/graphite](https://github.com/kyonyhk/graphite)
A self-improving agent on top of Carbon: use, reflect, recall. Persistent filesystem memory, not RAG.

**everyblock** · [github.com/kyonyhk/everyblock](https://github.com/kyonyhk/everyblock)
Every Singapore HDB resale since 1990, nearly a million transactions on an explorable 3D map. Real building footprints, a 36-year time scrubber, and offline question answering. Vite, MapLibre, deck.gl, no backend.

**Frost** · [frost-snow.com](https://www.frost-snow.com)
An immersive snowboarding brand site built with Three.js, WebGL, and GSAP. Recognized by Awwwards, FWA, and the GSAP showcase.

**Rabbit Hole** · [rabbit-hole.lovable.app](https://rabbit-hole.lovable.app/)
An AI ideation tool that turns ideas into a living visual map. Top 15 in the Lovable Build Competition.

**Genopets**
Product design lead for a move-to-earn crypto game that reached a million users. Led UX across web and mobile, including a 3D NFT minting experience.

> My product work at Akarii and Omaru lives in private repos. Carbon and Graphite are public, and show how I think about agent design from the ground up.
