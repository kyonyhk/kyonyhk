# Kuoloon Chong

**Product Designer / Design Engineer**

I'm a designer who architects AI agents. The problem I care about most is the balance between the deterministic harness and the non-deterministic model: how much to constrain it, what the agent remembers, when it acts, and how it shows its work. I think that balance is where good agents are won or lost.

## Selected work

**Akarii** · [www.akarii.ai](https://www.akarii.ai)
An AI-native team workspace. An AI teammate that observes team conversations, surfaces cited decisions, and executes work in the cloud. Built solo across product, design, and engineering.

**Omaru** · private alpha
An AI business partner inside a browser-based desktop OS that helps people turn their skills into a sellable service business. Co-founded; I lead onboarding, business setup, and the project lifecycle.

**Frost** · [frost-snow.com](https://www.frost-snow.com)
An immersive snowboarding brand site built with Three.js, WebGL, and GSAP. Recognized by Awwwards, FWA, and the GSAP showcase.

**Rabbit Hole** · [rabbit-hole.app](https://rabbit-hole.app/)
An AI thinking tool that interrogates the ideas you already have, surfacing the assumptions, biases, and dependencies they hide. Top 15 in the Lovable Build Competition.

**Genopets**
Product design lead for a move-to-earn crypto game that reached a million users. Led UX across web and mobile, including a 3D NFT minting experience.
